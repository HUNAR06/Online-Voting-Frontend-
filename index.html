Voter Rules & Regulations in India

Here are the key rules and regulations for voters in India:

  • Eligibility: Indian citizen, 18+ years old, resident of the constituency.
  • Voter ID: Must be registered in the electoral roll (Voter ID is recommended but not mandatory).
  • Voting Process: Done using Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs).
  • Disqualifications: Unsound mind, electoral offenses, criminal disqualifications.
  • Secret Ballot: Votes are confidential.
  • Code of Conduct: No bribing, hate speech, or influencing voters.
  • Options: Voters can choose NOTA (None of the Above).

For more details, visit the Election Commission of India.
